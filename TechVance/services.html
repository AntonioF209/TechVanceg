Welcome to GradeMaster!

Your Future Academic Success Companion

Home Screen

Welcome to the GradeMaster Home Screen, your gateway to academic success, all within a minimalist interface. Designed with simplicity and efficiency in mind, our app provides you with a clean and uncluttered layout, focusing on the essential elements to help you stay organized and informed.

Welcome to GradeMaster, where simplicity meets academic excellence. On our Home Screen, you'll find a clear display of your overall average, providing a quick snapshot of your academic performance. Discover your Best and Worst Subjects, empowering you to allocate your time efficiently. Our navigation bar with three sections ensures easy access to essential features: Grades, Home, and Settings.

In the Grades section, explore a detailed breakdown of subjects, assignments, and exams to make informed decisions about your study strategies. The Home section offers a streamlined overview of crucial metrics, keeping your focus intact with a minimalist design. Customize GradeMaster in the Settings section to seamlessly integrate into your routine.

Grades

Welcome to the Grade Section of our app, where you can easily keep track of your academic progress. With our user-friendly interface, you'll have all the tools you need to monitor your grades and stay on top of your education.

One of the key components of the Grade Section is the simple display of your overall average. With a quick glance, you can assess your academic performance and stay motivated to reach your goals. We believe that visualizing your progress is crucial for your success.

Your chosen subjects take center stage in the Grade Section. Presented in sleek and intuitive rectangles, each subject represents a unique aspect of your education. By pressing on a subject, you'll be seamlessly transported to a dedicated page where you can delve deeper into your individual performance for that subject.

On the subject-specific page, you'll find a comprehensive section to customize and fine-tune your grades. We understand that different evaluation components play a significant role in your academic journey. Therefore, we provide separate sections for your written and oral grades.

To ensure that your grades accurately reflect the weightage given to each evaluation component, we have incorporated adjustable sliders under each section. By moving the sliders, you can easily modify the weight of your written and oral grades, aligning them with your institution's grading system or personal preferences.

Once you've set the desired weight for your grades, our app generates color-coded grades that make it effortless for you to identify and add your scores. Simply press on the appropriate color-coded grade, and it will be automatically recorded as your grade for that specific component.

In our Grade Section, you can effortlessly handle multiple exams or assessments for a subject. Simply add multiple grades to the respective sections, and the app will adapt them to the chosen weight. Our goal is to provide flexibility and accuracy in tracking your performance across various evaluations.

But we haven't stopped there! We understand that your academic pursuits may extend beyond the subjects initially provided. That's why we've included an "Add More" button to cater to your diverse interests. By pressing this button, you have the freedom to add more subjects of your liking, ensuring that your educational journey is fully represented within our app.
